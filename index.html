Offensive Security Strategies

Red Team Tactics and Techniques

Red Teaming involves simulating adversary tactics to uncover detection capabilities and real defensive effectiveness. Integrates with existing security tools and platforms to validate coverage.

Social Engineering:

OSINT attacks utilize a comprehensive understanding of potential attack vectors such as physical attacks, social configurations, and publicly available data. Leverage open-source intelligence to create detailed profiles of target systems.

Phishing Simulation:

Focuses on applying known vulnerabilities, exploiting those vectors to infiltrate. Target testing protocols, authentication mechanisms, and responsive defense mechanisms, such as spam filtering and generic campaigns, to evaluate awareness and training effectiveness.

Internal Network Penetration:

An internal network penetration test aims to simulate insider threat or an organization. The objective is to assess the security measures in place within the network and identify potential vulnerabilities that could be exploited by attackers.
